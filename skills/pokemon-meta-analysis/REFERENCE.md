## Matchup Matrix

**Favorable:**

```
Fighting (Mega Lucario) → Darkness (Mega Absol variants)
Lightning (Manectric/Magnezone) → Water (Suicune/Greninja)
Water (Chien-Pao/Suicune) → Fire (Charizard/Blaziken)
Grass (Mega Sceptile) → Water (Suicune/Baxcalibur)
Darkness (Absol/Hydreigon) → Psychic (Altaria without partners)
```

**Difficult:**

```
Fire ← Water (dominant Water builds)
Water ← Lightning (Magnezone/Mega Manectric)
Darkness ← Fighting (Mega Lucario counters Absol)
Grass ← Fire (Mega Blaziken/Charizard)
```

## Rising Strategies

- Fighting-type dominance (Mega Lucario ex + Hitmontop + Igglybuff sleep)
- Mega Sceptile ex pushing Grass into relevance (140 effective dmg + Poison)
- Magnezone Oricorio as top-tier self-sufficient Lightning combo
- Dark-type diversity (4+ viable Darkness decks in A tier)
- Mega Altaria ex partner flexibility (Igglybuff/Greninja/Gourgeist all S tier)
- Paradox Pokemon (B3a Koraidon/Miraidon/Flutter Mane) — early evaluation phase

## Declining Strategies

- Pure basic Pokemon rush (outpaced by Mega + partner combos)
- Mono-type without partner synergy or free damage
- High-energy attackers without acceleration (4+ cost)
- Decks without bench manipulation or free damage
- Mega Scizor ex Revavroom (fallen out of favor)
- Bellibolt ex Magnezone (superseded by Magnezone Oricorio)
