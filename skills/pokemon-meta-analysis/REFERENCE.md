## Matchup Matrix

**Favorable:**

```
Lightning (Miraidon/Magnezone) → Water (Suicune/Baxcalibur/Milotic)
Water (Chien-Pao/Suicune)      → Fire (Blaziken/Charizard)
Grass (Mega Sceptile ex)       → Water (Suicune/Baxcalibur)
Psychic (Mega Altaria ex / Iron Valiant) → Fighting (Mega Lucario — now mid tier)
Darkness (Hydreigon/Mega Absol) → Psychic basics without partners
```

**Difficult:**

```
Fighting ← Psychic (Mega Altaria ex / Iron Valiant now dominant)
Fire      ← Water (dominant Water builds)
Water     ← Lightning (Miraidon ex + Magnezone are tier-1)
Grass     ← Fire
```

## Rising Strategies

- **Paradox Pokémon define the meta.** Iron Valiant (B3a) is the partner in the #1 deck; Iron Boulder/Crown/Moth/Leaves proliferate Psychic and Fire builds.
- **Miraidon ex (B3a) spawned a whole Lightning family** (Magnezone+Miraidon ex, Miraidon ex+Magnezone/Pichu) via Hadron Ray's per-Lightning-energy scaling — Lightning is now tier-1.
- **Mega Altaria ex stays S-tier by pivoting partners** to Iron Valiant / Espeon / Gourgeist, away from the now-fallen Igglybuff and Greninja lines.
- **Mega Sceptile ex keeps Grass top-tier** (Terminating Tail 130 + Poison = 140 effective).
- Darkness remains diverse (Hydreigon / Zoroark / Giratina all B-tier).

## Declining Strategies

- **Mega Lucario ex + Hitmontop**: collapsed from S to C/D — Fighting is no longer dominant.
- **Mega Altaria ex + Igglybuff / Greninja**: fell from S to C — sleep stall and Greninja snipe are outpaced.
- **Flutter Mane ex**: F-tier (~36% WR) — overhyped Paradox that underdelivered.
- **Magnezone + Oricorio**: superseded by Magnezone + Miraidon ex variants.
- High-energy attackers without Miraidon/Magnezone/Baxcalibur acceleration.
- Pure basic-Pokémon rush without partner synergy or free damage.
