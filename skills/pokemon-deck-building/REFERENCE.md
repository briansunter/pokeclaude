## Common Mistakes

- Running 3+ energy types (too inconsistent)
- Missing Rare Candy in Stage 2 decks
- Not including Cyrus (most important gust effect)
- Overloading high-energy attackers without acceleration
- Ignoring bench size planning
- Not planning point maps (1-1-3 for Mega decks)
- Forgetting Mega Pokemon are worth 3 points (need cheap 1-pt KO plan)
